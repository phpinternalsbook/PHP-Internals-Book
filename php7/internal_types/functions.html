Functions¶

The body of PHP functions are represented with the zend_function structure. However, handling them is rarely done as they are solely needed for the VM. In general PHP callable s are what will need to be dealt with, which are represented by the pair of zend_fcall_info/zend_fcall_info_cache structures.
